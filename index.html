Summary

The purpose of this project was to compare the changes in weather at many different latitudes up and down the Northern and Southern Hemispheres. The data consisted of over 500 cities and was sourced from the OpenWeatherMap API. We relied on Matplotlib and Pandas modules to organize the data and create the visualizations. The plots compare max temperature, humidity, cloudiness, and wind speed to each city's latitude. The purpose of this site is to present the data, visualitions, and analyses we found.
