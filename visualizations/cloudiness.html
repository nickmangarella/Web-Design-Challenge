Cloudiness

This visual shows how most of the cities within our dataset saw either a mostly cloudy day or no clouds out all. There is quite a spread in the middle which could indicate that different cities are in currently in very different seasons or currently experience storms. It seems there is no clear correlation between cloudiness and latitude.
