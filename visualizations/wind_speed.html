Wind Speed

Interestingly enough all of the cities experienced at least mild wind on this day with quite a few outliers above 25 mph. Many factors play a role in wind speed especially the usual present local conditions, geographical features of the location, and whether or not storms are present in the area. Something to note is that the outliers for this visual appear to exist at the outermost latitudes and those near the equator, however the data isn't conclusive enough to say these latitudes experience inherently higher wind speeds.
